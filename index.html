AKAN NAMES

Children of akan people of ghana,togo and ivory coast are often given names which are based on the day of the week they are born.These day name have additional meanings conserning the 'kra'which is the soul,personality and character of the person.In the akan tradition. it is believed that every child comes into this world with a name which is part of there soul, and the soul determines the day they want to come into this world

ABOUT THIS APP

This is a web application that takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born also depending on their gender then it outputs there akan names.
